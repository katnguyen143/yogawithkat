welcome

hi, i'm kat!

I’m a yoga teacher and acrobat based in Belmont, California. My teaching journey began in 2024 with Outsiders, an outdoor recreation club at UC Berkeley, where I led free weekly yoga classes for BIPOC students. After completing my 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training at Bali Yoga Ashram in June 2025, I began teaching internationally — first in Japan, offering university yoga classes and intimate tatami-room sessions, and later in Thailand.

In Koh Phangan, I taught at Nestra Beach Shala, a community-centered space offering free yoga and English classes to Thai and Burmese locals. I also co-taught all-levels Acroyoga at The Tipi and offered private sessions tailored to individual needs. These experiences across Asia deepened my understanding of accessible movement and community-led practice.

I’m now back in the Bay Area, where I currently teach at Ladera Recreation District in Portola Valley, lead classes in parks and outdoor spaces, and continue building community through warm, intentional movement. From calming yang-to-yin sunset flows to energizing Ashtanga-Vinyasa handstand and inversion practices, I teach to cultivate breath, presence, and connection — and I believe yoga should be accessible to everyone. ♡

current offerings

yoga and acroyoga classes

group yoga classes

My group classes are designed to be welcoming, dynamic, and accessible to all levels. Whether you're brand new to yoga or looking to deepen your practice, you'll be supported with clear guidance, breath cues, and optional props. Expect a mix of strength-building movement, breathwork, and space to move at your own pace.

donation-based yoga classes

Yoga was never meant to be a privilege, yet financial barriers often make it feel that way. These classes are offered on a donation basis to make yoga more accessible, especially for BIPOC and local communities. All levels are welcome. Pay what you can, and know that your presence is enough.

private acroyoga classes

Learn the foundations of acro or dive into flows, whips, pops, and hand-to-hand. As a seasoned flyer, I specialize in helping bases build strength, balance, and clear communication. Whether you're just starting out or refining your technique, we’ll co-create trust and flow at your own pace.
