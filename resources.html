wondering where to begin?

yoga resources ♡

I remember the feeling of taking my first class — feeling inspired, then coming home and getting totally overwhelmed by the sea of YouTube channels and styles of yoga. It was hard to know what was right for my body, my energy, or my experience level.

So I’ve gathered a few beginner-friendly flows and tips to help you get started with your yoga journey. ♡

🌞 Sun Salutations

Sun Salutations (Surya Namaskar) are a foundational flow that gently warms up the body and introduces key poses. If you only have enough time for 10 minutes of yoga, try 3–5 rounds for a complete mini practice.

Once you feel comfortable with foundational movements, explore flows that develop strength and joint stability while keeping your practice grounded and balanced.

🌱 Kat’s Tips for New Yogis

  • Start slow. Even 10 minutes or 3 rounds of Sun Salutations can go a long way.
  • Focus on the breath before the movements.
  • Use props such as cushions, books, or rolled towels.
  • Modify freely. Yoga meets you where you are.
  • Don’t worry about looking “right” — focus on how it feels.
  • Practice kindness toward your body, your breath, and your process. Everyone’s journey looks different.
