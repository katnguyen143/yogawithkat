wondering where to begin?

yoga resources ♡

I remember the feeling of taking my first class. Feeling inspired, then coming home and getting totally overwhelmed by the sea of YouTube channels and different styles of yoga. It was hard to know what was right for my body, my energy, or my experience level.

So I’ve gathered a few beginner-friendly resources and tips below to help you get started with your yoga journey. ♡

sun salutations

Sun Salutations (Surya Namaskar) are a foundational flow that gently warms up the body and introduces key poses. If you only have enough time for 10 minutes of yoga, I recommend using the time to practice 3-5 rounds of Sun Salutations.

  • 10-minute Yoga for Beginners
  • 20-minute Flexibility & Mobility Flow

gentle full-body flows

These gentle flows are great for building comfort, body awareness, and breath connection.

  • 30-minute Stretch (No Props)
  • Flexibility, Strength & Balance

build strength & mobility

Once you feel more comfortable with basic poses and movements, these flows help develop strength and joint support without overwhelming your body.

  • Full Body Strength & Flexibility
  • Gentle Flexibility Practice

kat's tips for new yogis

  • Start slow. Even 10 minutes or 3 rounds of Sun Salutations can go a long way!
  • Focus on the breath first, then the movements
  • Use props such as cushions, books, or rolled towels
  • Modify freely. Yoga meets you where you are
  • Don’t worry about looking “right,” focus on how it feels
  • Practice kindness toward your body, your breath, and your process. Everyone's journey looks different
